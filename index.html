My story

Hello, My name is Marc Fieser. I was born and raised in Woodinville, Washington and graduated from Woodinville Highschool in 2003. A year later, I joined the US Army and served for nine years until June of 2013. After completing my time in service, I returned home and attended the University of Washington utilizing my GI Bill and I graduated and received my degree in computer science and software enginnering in june of 2013. I am Now a student at code fellows where I am learning to develop web applications.
